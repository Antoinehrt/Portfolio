Training program

Fast AI Ateliers Créactifs (10/2024 - 11/2024)



I participated in a training program on Artificial Intelligence organized by Ateliers Créactifs, which focused on building, training, and evaluating AI models. The program emphasized hands-on learning with practical applications, enabling me to understand and apply cutting-edge techniques in machine learning and neural networks.
Key Learnings
During the training, I gained expertise in managing data pipelines, training machine learning models, and evaluating their performance. I worked on tasks such as preparing datasets, splitting them into training and validation subsets, and creating efficient workflows using DataLoaders. I explored the advantages of transfer learning by using the pre-trained ResNet34 model and fine-tuning it with application-specific data. This approach significantly enhanced the model’s accuracy compared to training a model from scratch. Additionally, I delved into performance evaluation, using precision as a key metric to measure and compare the effectiveness of pre-trained and non-pre-trained models.
Skills Acquired
The training equipped me with valuable skills in data manipulation, transfer learning, and performance evaluation, as well as a solid understanding of how to apply pre-trained neural networks to real-world problems. These insights have broadened my expertise in machine learning and prepared me to address practical AI challenges with confidence.
