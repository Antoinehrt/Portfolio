Training program

Dutch Forem (04/2025 - 10/2025)



I participated in a Dutch language training program at the Forem training center in Mons. I completed three consecutive courses, starting at level A0, without any prior knowledge of Dutch. By the end of the first course, I reached level A1, then progressed to level A2 after the second course, and finally achieved level B1 upon completing the third course. Each level was certified by the Forem through the ELAO testing system.
Key Learnings
Throughout the program, I developed strong skills in understanding and communicating in Dutch. I learned to construct sentences, expand vocabulary, and improve listening, reading, writing, and speaking abilities. The program provided a structured and progressive approach, enabling me to gain confidence in practical, everyday use of the language.
Skills Acquired
By the end of the training, I acquired certified proficiency in Dutch at level B1. I can now communicate effectively in a variety of professional and social situations, demonstrating a solid foundation in both written and spoken Dutch.
